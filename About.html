TourX acts much like a tour guide, only this one works on your schedule.
A virtual tour is a sequence of panoramic images that are ‘stitched’ together to create a ‘virtual’ experience of any location. Once created, the viewer is able to experience what it is like to be somewhere they are actually not. This virtual experience could be viewed through their desktop computers, their laptops, their tablets and even mobile devices.

TourX is a real-life experience! This acts like a tour guide in the real world and can be used for tourism, university tours, relocation Where-to-live tours and even personal heritage tours. We have the points of interest to see what you may expect from each tour before taking it.
